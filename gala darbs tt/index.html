(Some)Playable Characters

Abrams

Abrams is a tank, specializes in close range combat.

Bebop

Bebop is a scrap golem, can pull enemies with a hook, place bombs on them, exploding uppercut them away.

Billy

Billy is a tank type(less than others), specializing in close range combat and high burst damage.

Calico

Calico is an assasin type, specializing in escaping and extremely high burst damage.

The Doorman

The Doorman is a ambush, support type. A self proclaimed "Fledgling god", utilizes good crowd control, high burst damage, and movement using portal doors.

Drifter

Drifter is a hunter, specializing in 1 to 1 combat, having abilities that stalk singular targets.

Dynamo

Dynamo is a support type, knocking enemies in the air, healing allies, helping allies escape from enemies, having a singularity ultimate, that can wipe an entire team.

Grey Talon

Grey talon is a sniper type, having very high burst damage from long distances, also having little health.

Haze

Haze is an assasin type, having the ability to make enemies sleep, can go invisible, and an ability that excels in prolonmged 1 to 1 combat.

Viscous

Viscous is a versatile character, having the ability to focus on helping allies, to be vexing for enemies with poke damage, or go for an high movement, high burst damage build.
